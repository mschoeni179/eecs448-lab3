Yuzuru Hanyu



Yuzuru Hanyu is a figure skater from Japan. He is most famous for winning the gold medal for the Men's Singles event in the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi and the 2018 Winter Olympics in Pyeongchang. Yuzuru Hanyu was the first man since Dick Button won back-to-back gold medals in the 1952 and 1948 Olympics for the men's event.
